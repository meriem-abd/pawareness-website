did you know?

Mice and rats often chew through car cables, causing expensive damage and can spread dangerous diseases in urban areas. Stray cats play a vital role in keeping rodent populations under control, protecting both our health and property.



what do they struggle with?

rough weather conditions

Cats struggle to find shelter from rain, harsh sun, and cold winds... and with increasing urbanization and fewer green spaces we have destroyed the natural shelters they once relied on

Lack of Food & Clean Water

Stray cats hardly find food/water, as they often rely on scraps, trash, or inconsistent feeding. Poor diets lead to sickness, weakness, and malnutrition.

Kittens separated from their Moms

Rabies

Risk: Extremely High

üîÑ

Symptoms:

Sudden behavior changes, drooling, trouble swallowing, paralysis, seizures, then coma and death.

Cause:

Rabies virus (usually transmitted through bites from infected animals)

Treatment:

No cure once symptoms appear (in both cats and humans). Immediate post-exposure treatment for humans if bitten or scratched. Vaccination for prevention.
