Why Pawareness?

Every day, countless cats are abandoned, misunderstood, or left to survive on the streets, not because people don’t care, but because they don’t know.
Pawareness exists to change that: to educate, inspire, and connect people with these gentle, beautiful souls who deserve safety and love.

The Change Starts With You

With just a little awareness, we can prevent suffering, encourage compassion, and build safer, kinder communities for cats and for people alike.
