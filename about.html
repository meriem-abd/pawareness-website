Hi! I am Meriem, the founder of Pawareness

I am a student, coder, English educator and interpreter, and an advocate for education and animal welfare. I am also the founder of Pawareness Initiative. Beyond coding and academics, I build shelters, provide care, and educate my community about environmental responsibility, combining technology with hands-on action to create a better world for all living beings.

My Philosophy

I believe in rescuing stray cats by respecting their natural habitat instead of confining them to shelters. Through kindness, education, and sustainability efforts like recycling and cleanups, I work to protect both animals and the environment, and foster a healthy human–feline coexistence.
