treating eye infection

This page shows how to gently clean and care for stray kittens’ eyes. With simple steps, you can ease their pain, prevent damage, and give them the chance to see the world clearly again

⚠️ Why Treat Eye Infections Fast?

Untreated infections can cause pain, blindness, or spread to other kittens. Quick cleaning prevents damage and helps the kitten recover safely.

materials

cotton pads

salt

water

Prepare a weak saline solution by mixing 1 cup of boiled, cooled water with ¼ teaspoon of salt

Dip a clean cotton pad in warm salty water

slowly open the cats eye with fingers

Wipe the eye from the inner corner outward, using a fresh pad for each wipe.
Repeat daily
