make a cats water bowl

This simple craft turns a used plastic bottle or jug into a lifesaving water bowl for stray cats. Access to clean drinking water is one of the biggest daily challenges for street cats, especially in hot weather. By placing water bowls in safe, shaded spots, you help prevent dehydration and improve their overall health.
