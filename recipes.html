feed cats

This page shares simple, affordable recipes to prepare nutritious meals for stray cats. Using low-cost or free ingredients like market scraps and pantry staples

20% filler

A balanced mix of rice, pasta, or vegetables to provide energy, fiber, and essential vitamins for healthy digestion

80% protein

A rich portion of chicken, fish, or eggs to supply the essential proteins and amino acids cats need to stay strong and healthy

where to gather ingredients from?

Ask your local butcher for chicken or meat scraps â€” skin, cartilage, and small bits of meat. Most of the time, they give them for free since they would be thrown away anyway.

Visit a local fish market or talk to fishermen. Fish heads, bones, and leftover pieces are often discarded, but they make a great protein source for stray cats.

healthy Recipes that cats love

carrots
zucchini
fish (serdine)

Boil carrot and zucchini until soft.
Mash them together into a smooth puree.
Add shreded sardine and Mix well and cool before serving.

white rice
chicken scraps (skin, cartilage, wings)

Boil chicken scraps in water until fully cooked.
Strain the meat and shred into small pieces.
Cook rice in the chicken broth for extra flavor.
Mix shredded chicken into rice.

pasta
eggs
canned tuna

Boil pasta and eggs until soft, drain well
drain tuna from excess oil
mix pasta, shredded eggs, and tuna well
