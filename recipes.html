This page shares simple, affordable recipes to prepare nutritious meals for stray cats. Using low-cost or free ingredients like market scraps and pantry staples

20% filler

A balanced mix of rice, pasta, or vegetables to provide energy, fiber, and essential vitamins for healthy digestion

80% protein

A rich portion of chicken, fish, or eggs to supply the essential proteins and amino acids cats need to stay strong and healthy

Healthy Recipes that Cats Love

Ingredients:

  • Carrots
  • Zucchini
  • Fish (sardine)

Steps:

  1. Boil carrot and zucchini until soft.
  2. Mash them together into a smooth puree.
  3. Add shredded sardine and mix well.
  4. Cool before serving.

Ingredients:

  • White rice
  • Chicken scraps (skin, cartilage, wings)

Steps:

  1. Boil chicken scraps in water until fully cooked.
  2. Strain the meat and shred into small pieces.
  3. Cook rice in the chicken broth for extra flavor.
  4. Mix shredded chicken into rice.

Ingredients:

  • Pasta
  • Eggs
  • Canned tuna

Steps:

  1. Boil pasta and eggs until soft, then drain well.
  2. Drain tuna from excess oil.
  3. Mix pasta, shredded eggs, and tuna well.
